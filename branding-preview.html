Long Multiplication

Section 1.1

To multiply large numbers, we can break the problem down:

  • Align numbers by their place value (units, tens, hundreds).
  • Multiply the top number by each digit of the bottom number.
  • Always remember to add a placeholder zero when moving to the tens column.
Key Tip Keep your columns straight! Misaligned columns are the most common cause of errors in the exam.
